Advaita

Advaita is the Annual Techno-Cult Fest of IIIT Bhubaneshwar. Started as a modest initiative, Advaita has grown into a popular brand. It is a gathering of the intelligentsia and the erudite. It is a platform for the talented and gifted souls. A plaform where imagination and talent coalesce to conceive inspiration.

Advaita has expanded its reach and established itself as one of the biggest and most anticipated events in Bhubaneswar. The previous editions have gained unexpectedly huge responses in the form of participation from all over India. It is a plethora of events like Technotronic, Pronites et cetera. We hope to make the upcoming edition of Advaita a grand success and gift cherishable moments to one and all.
